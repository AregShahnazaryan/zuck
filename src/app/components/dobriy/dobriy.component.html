Part of the Coca-Cola HBC, Добрый is the leader of the natural juice market in Russia and a number of other countries, including Armenia, where it is among the most recognized brands. However, until recently, the brand was practically non-existent on Armenian social media. Our goal was to build social media presence for Добрый Armenia with a view of building brand equity through highly targeted communications on Facebook, which was identified by initial market research to be the most popular social media platform for Добрый’s target audience.

Kindness is natural. So are Добрый juices. Building on two key brand pillars - kindness and naturalness - we developed our social media strategy around four directions:

  • Raising general brand awareness and brand recognition
  • Building a strong association between the Добрый brand and the concept of kindness
  • Positioning Добрый as the first choice for family consumption
  • Positioning Добрый для детей as the first choice for children
