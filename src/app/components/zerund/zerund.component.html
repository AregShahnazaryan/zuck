Just another fancy name or generation that comes to change the world... Before going forward to the mission of Z-generation (next Gen Z), shortly about the origin. Gen Z is a demographic group born between 1995 and 2005. According to numerous statistics, in 2025 they will make up the majority of the consumer population, about 85%. Today, many brands from the world are developing their strategy in such a way as to please Gen Z.

Remarkable thing is that Gen Z is committed to globalization. They are against all kinds of discrimination, consistent concerning the environment (as evidenced by the protocols on plastics) and generally are more active in social networks. But that doesn't mean they depend on social media.

On the contrary, they use the Internet for self-education, earning money and, finally, for self-expression and voicing problems.
