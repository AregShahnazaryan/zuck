Rhinoplasty is among the most demanded beauty and surgical services in Armenia and Dr. Gevorg Yeghiazaryan’s QIT.AM brand is one of the leaders of Armenia’s rhinoplasty market.

While Dr. Gevorg was a narrowly recognized professional in the healthcare field, he was not known to the general population.

Our goal was to turn Dr. Gevorg into a nationwide celebrity and to break stigmas around rhinoplasty, so that no woman is judged for getting a nose-job.

The eyes are mom’s, the nose is Gevorg Yeghiazaryan’s

Across the world, people compare children to parents with phrases like “She has her mother’s eyes” … In Armenia, we say “the eyes are mom’s”.

The slogan is a familiar phrase with strong social currency due to humor and sexual subtext (does her Dad know the nose is someone else’s?).

Billboards advertising nose-jobs are not a common sight in Armenia. For maximum impact, we chose billboards as primary media, placing 23 of them in Yerevan’s busiest streets for 60 days.

outcome

Gevorg Yeghiazaryan was a household name within one night.
The campaign grew viral after an hour of going live. Armenian social media was stormed by thousands of posts, comments, shares, and memes, with many brands and celebrities joining the wave.

The online hype led to offline results – 1400% increase in client inquiries.

More importantly, for the first time in Armenia, we created an honest discussion about rhinoplasty. With women proudly joining the conversation and owning their decisions to get nose-jobs, we broke the stereotype that surgeries are to be ashamed of.

The campaign became one of the most-discussed and widest-spread ad campaigns in Armenia and was shortlisted in two international ad festivals - Red Apple 2019 and Ad Black Sea 2019.
