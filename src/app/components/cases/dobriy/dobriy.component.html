Annual SM Promotion for Добрый

  • Brand
    Добрый Armenia
  • Date
    2018-2019
  • Industry
    FMCG
  • Medium
    Social Media

Part of the Coca-Cola HBC, Добрый is the leader of the natural juice market in Russia and a number of other countries, including Armenia, where it is among the most recognized brands.

However, until recently, the brand was practically non-existent on Armenian social media.

Our goal was to build social media presence for Добрый Armenia with a view of building brand equity through highly targeted communications on Facebook, which was identified by initial market research to be the most popular social media platform for Добрый’s target audience.

Kindness is natural. So are Добрый juices. Building on two key brand pillars - kindness and naturalness - we developed our social media strategy around four directions:


  • Raising general brand awareness and brand recognition
  • Building a strong association between the Добрый brand and the concept of kindness
  • Positioning Добрый as the first choice for family consumption
  • Positioning Добрый для детей as the first choice for children

From regular posts to interactives, situational ads, photo stories, giveaways and memes, all content was inspired by local insights and revolved around the concepts of kindness and nature, building and strengthening the brand’s positioning.

In addition to regular content, several short-term campaigns were carried out, including the Christmas Campaign, Back to School Campaign, Kindness Day Campaign, etc., as well as a long-term campaign promoting the 100% Natural Line.

outcome

Due to consistent, unusual and highly targeted communications, Добрый Armenia’s Facebook page quickly became one of the most engaging social media accounts run by a brand. By forming a progressively expanding community primarily composed of parents looking for natural and nutritious products, we were able to


  • Steadily increase brand awareness throughout the duration of the social media promotion.
  • Steadily increase the brand’s love score throughout the duration of the social media promotion.
  • Increase sales across all product categories.
  • Build brand equity.
