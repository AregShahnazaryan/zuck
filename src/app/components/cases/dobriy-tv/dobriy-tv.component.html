Part of the Coca-Cola HBC, Добрый is the leader of the natural juice market in Russia and a number of other countries, including Armenia, where it is among the most recognized brands.

In 2019, Добрый released a new line of 100% natural juices.

Our main goal was to raise awareness about the new line among the target audience, build brand equity and develop a consumption habit.

100% Natural, 100% Kind - Добрый’s first TVC written and produced in Armenia․ The ad features the new 100% natural juices in the perfect family scene: Dad comes home to children preparing food bowls for street animals. To reward them for their selfless kindness, he brings them their favorite juice.

Our strategy was to position the Добрый 100% natural juice line as the ultimate family beverage for health and nutrition conscious families, while reinforcing the association between the Добрый brand and the concept of kindness.

Taking into account the media habits of the core target group - married men and women with children - television was selected as the primary media for the campaign, with the Dobry Armenia Facebook page acting as a support channel.

From big decisions like selecting the location, casting the actors and choosing the music to the most minute details like styling and make-up, all execution was focused on creating an atmosphere of a healthy and happy family.

outcome

Showing fast results in terms of both brand image and increased sales, the ad aired on leading Armenian TV Channels for over 8 months and served as the primary brand communication for the year.
