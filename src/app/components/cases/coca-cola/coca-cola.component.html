Annual Social Media Promotion for Coca-Cola HBC

Coca-Cola Hellenic Bottling Company Armenia is one of the world’s largest bottlers of The Coca-Cola Company and represents a portfolio of 10 worldwide brands, including Coca-Cola, Fanta, Sprite, Добрый, Bonaqua, etc.

Since its launch in Armenia, the company engaged in CSR activities like environmental protection, sustainable development, youth education and community engagement. However, due to its passive presence on social media, the CSR efforts of the company were largely unnoticed by its target group.

The company was famous for bottling world-famous beverages. Our task was to make it also famous for being a socially responsible company.

In order to change public perceptions of the company, we focused our social media strategy on 5 key directions:


  • Positioning Coca-Cola as a socially responsible brand
  • Raising public awareness of and interest in the brand’s CSR mission
  • Ensuring public participation in the brand’s CSR activities
  • Enhancing the image of the brand among its target audience
  • Positioning Coca-Cola as a socially responsible employer

When developing the page content, we primarily focused on showing the real impact of the brand’s CSR efforts through beneficiaries’ personal stories, testimonials, survey results, numbers and statistics, photo and video reports covering CSR events and activities, etc.

To balance out the serious and complex messages conveyed by the page, we opted for a light and straightforward visual identity, integrating iconic Coca-Cola symbols and images into minimalistic illustrations.

All content posted on the page, including posts not directly related to the brand’s CSR mission, such as situational ads and holiday posts, featured themes of social responsibility.

To further enhance the image of the brand, the page content not only focused on the brand’s CSR activities, but also raised awareness of issues important to the brand, such as environmental protection, sustainable development and lifelong education.

To make the content more emotional and personal, we used real photos and quotes of company employees.

outcome

In addition to a manifold increase in across all key performance indicators (page likes, reach, impressions, etc.), we significantly increased the visibility of Coca-Cola’s CSR activities, thus not only ensuring public awareness of the company’s socially responsible efforts, but also increasing their impact.

Throughout the year, we helped the company ensure active public participation in its events and activities – trainings, workshops, cleaning days and opening events. Here is just one case of how social media activation contributed to the success of the brand’s CSR work.
