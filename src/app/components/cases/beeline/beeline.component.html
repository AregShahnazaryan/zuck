Part of the Coca-Cola HBC, Добрый is the leader of the natural juice market in Russia and a number of other countries, including Armenia, where it is among the most recognized brands.

However, until recently, the brand was practically non-existent on Armenian social media.

Our goal was to build social media presence for Добрый Armenia with a view of building brand equity through highly targeted communications on Facebook, which was identified by initial market research to be the most popular social media platform for Добрый’s target audience.

A Suitcase Full of Internet Roaming

Armenians are heavy packers. Even when we’re going somewhere for just a few days, we get there with a fully-packed suitcase, especially since many items of everyday use are much cheaper in Armenia than overseas.

The simple slogan communicates a complex message - Beeline Roaming is so cheap, that it’s better to pack it with you than look for internet or mobile connection abroad.

Take a walk in Yerevan at any time, and you’ll be sure to see a lot of Beeline ads. Most of them feature photos and a little bit of graphics. Same goes for most other outdoor ads in the telecommunications industry.

To make our billboards and scrolling posters stand out and to appeal to the younger generation, we opted for modern and colorful illustrations.

To make sure our ad targets travelers specifically, we decided to use the suitcase (of course, in Beeline’s signature yellow color) as the key visual trigger.

Finally, since Georgia was the country of destination for a large segment of our target group, we decided to create a separate visual for this specific group.

Placed on the road to the Airport, the key visual integrates all the right triggers – the yellow suitcase with travel stickers, the departures terminal of the Airport and the collective image of our target group – a young, stylish traveler.

The second visual is placed on the road to the Armenia-Georgia border and features a situation familiar to everyone who has travelled to Georgia – a suitcase in the trunk of the car, near the border crossing.

outcome

Taking into account the feedback received from the target group and the increase of users of the advertised package, the campaign reached its goal of promoting Beeline’s roaming services among the target group.

In fact, the campaign was so effective, that Beeline decided to roll out a social media campaign based on the same concept, this time targeting travelers going to Italy – one of the most popular destinations for Armenians.
